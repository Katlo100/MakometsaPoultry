Who We Are

Makometsa Poultry is a proudly local poultry business based in Gabane, Botswana. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the highest quality poultry products at affordable prices. Founded with the goal of promoting food security and sustainable agriculture, we continue to grow through passion, integrity and commitment to our customers.

Our Mission

To supply households and businesses with quality poultry products while promoting ethical farming practices and empowering youth poultry farmers in Botswana.

Our Vision

To become one of the most reliable and trusted poultry supplier in Botswana, recognized for quality, consistency and innovation.

Our Values

At Makometsa Poultry, we are guided by a set of core values that shape our business and define who we are as a company.

These values include:

Quality

: We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality poultry products that meet their needs and exceed their expectations.

Integrity

: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency and respect for our customers, employees and the environment.

Customer Satisfaction

: We strive to provide excellent customer service and build long-term relationships with our customers based on trust and satisfaction.

Innovation

: We are constantly seeking new ways to improve our products, processes and services to better serve our customers and stay ahead of the competition.

Responsibility

: We are committed to operating our business in a responsible manner, considering the impact on our stakeholders and the environment.

Community

: We are dedicated to supporting our local community through job creation, education and charitable initiatives.

Affordability

: We believe that high-quality poultry products should be accessible to everyone, and we strive to offer competitive prices without compromising on quality.

Sustainability

: We are committed to sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and ensure the long-term viability of our business.

Our Commitment

At Makometsa Poultry, we are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality poultry products while promoting ethical farming practices and supporting our local community. We are dedicated to ensuring the health and well-being of our chickens, using sustainable farming methods, and providing excellent customer service. We believe in building long-term relationships with our customers based on trust, integrity and mutual respect.
